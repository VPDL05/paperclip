Why match your sensitivity?

Muscle memory is crucial for FPS games. If you play Valorant at one sensitivity and switch to Apex Legends with a different "cm/360" (centimeters per 360-degree turn), your aim will feel "off." This calculator ensures your mouse movement distance remains exactly the same across all engines.

Supported Games Logic

Different game engines use different "Yaw" values. For example, Source Engine games (CS:GO, Apex) usually share the same multiplier, while Unreal Engine games (Valorant, Fortnite) differ.
