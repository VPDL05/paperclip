What is the "Time Price"?

We often look at price tags in terms of currency, but the real currency of life is time. This calculator uses your "Real Hourly Wage" to determine how long you have to sit at your desk or stand at your job to afford a purchase.

The Math

If you input an annual salary, we divide it by 2,080 (the standard number of working hours in a year: 40 hours/week Ã— 52 weeks). We then divide the item price by this hourly rate to give you the "Time Cost."
