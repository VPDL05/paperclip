Why do Instagram Bios fail?

Instagram and TikTok interfaces often strip out "white space" to save screen room. If you type a list, they mash it into one sentence.

How this tool works

We use a special Unicode character called the "Zero Width Space" or "Braille Pattern Blank" depending on the context. When you click Copy, we insert these invisible anchors into your line breaks. This tricks the social media app into thinking there is content on the line, forcing it to keep the space open.
